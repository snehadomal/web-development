Black Edition

As part of its 40th anniversary celebrations, Maruti Suzuki has launched the Black Edition of the cars on sale through Nexa dealerships. This edition brings the Pearl Midnight Black shade for the Ciaz, Ignis, Baleno, XL6 and Grand Vitara. Nexa Black Edition available on all variants of Ciaz, Baleno. Black editions, sometimes referred to as ‘Midnight Editions’, or ‘Blackline Editions’ depending on manufacturer protocol, are specialist versions of high performance or luxury vehicles.
